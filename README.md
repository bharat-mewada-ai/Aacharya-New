Aacharya is a gamified wellness ecosystem built to tackle one of the biggest problems in fitness — lack of consistency.

Instead of just tracking workouts or calories, Aacharya focuses on behavior change. It combines AI-driven personalized plans with gamification elements like XP, levels, rewards, and avatars to keep users engaged long-term.

Key features include:

Personalized diet and workout plans
Habit tracking and consistency scoring
Gamified progression system (XP, ranks, rewards)
AI-powered insights and recommendations
Future scope: marketplace, integrations with wearables, and social challenges

The goal is simple: make discipline addictive.
